Our Story

Where Every Woman
Finds Her Radiance

Founded in Davao City, Claire's Bridal Couture was born from a simple belief: every woman deserves to feel extraordinary on the moments that matter most — without compromising on elegance or breaking the bank.

For over a decade, we have dressed brides, debutantes, and distinguished guests in gowns that speak of craftsmanship, culture, and grace. Our collection spans timeless bridal silhouettes to proud Filipiniana heritage wear — each piece maintained with meticulous care.

  • Elegant & Affordable Gown Rentals
  • Professional Fitting & Alterations
  • Weddings, Debuts & Formal Events
  • Cultural & Heritage Celebrations
  • Personalized Styling Consultation
